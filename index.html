Welcome to ActiveRecall

About ActiveRecall

ActiveRecall is a browser extension that helps you learn more effectively while watching educational videos.

Features

  • Set custom recall intervals
  • Voice input for recall notes
  • Easy sharing and copying of notes

Privacy Policy

ActiveRecall is committed to protecting your privacy. Here's how we handle your data:

  • We do not collect or store any personal information.
  • Your recall notes are stored locally in your browser and are not transmitted to our servers.
  • Voice input is processed locally and not sent to any external servers.
  • We use browser storage to save your preferences, which remains on your device.
  • The extension only activates on video platforms and does not access or interact with other websites.
  • We do not use any tracking or analytics tools.

By using ActiveRecall, you agree to this privacy policy. If you have any questions or concerns, please contact us.
